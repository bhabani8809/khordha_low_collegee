About College


The Story of KLC

Khordha Law College (KLC) is the statutory college recognized by the Govt. of Odisha under the Madhusudan Law University. The College is affiliated to Madhusudan Law University, Cuttack and recognized by the Bar Council of India (BCI).

KLC Features

Based in Khordha, KLC is an integral part of our vibrant city. With programs and partnerships in education, we're creating a new model of engagement for urban universities.

Study

Khordha Law College offers a unique opportunity to study law with a nation-wide academic reputation. The college endeavors towards bringing social, political and economic change with the help of law.
